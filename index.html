⭐ About Sara

Sara Costa is the founder and principal cloud security consultant at Papaya Kiwi Consulting LLC. With over seven years of hands-on experience across AWS, Azure, and GCP, Sara has built her career at the intersection of deep technical expertise and clear communication — making complex cloud security concepts understandable for everyone from engineers to executives.

Before founding Papaya Kiwi, Sara served as a primary SME for cloud posture management and data security posture at Palo Alto Networks, guiding enterprise customers through Prisma Cloud adoption. Her consultancy career spans Germany with BxC Security, where she delivered corporate PKI architecture and enterprise security governance, and IoT software development at BENTELER Group in Paderborn.

A proud Brazilian-born trilingual (English, Portuguese, German), Sara brings a global perspective to every engagement. She holds an active Secret clearance with a Top Secret investigation in progress, and actively serves in the US Army Reserves as a 25/35 Series MOS specialist in classified and unclassified technical environments.

Sara holds an M.S. in Computer and Information Systems Security from the University of Maryland Global Campus. Her patent-pending dual-agent remediation method — showcased at BSides South Florida in May 2026 — is the foundation of Papaya Kiwi's agentic cloud security approach.
